What are healthy habits?

Healthy habits are things you do for yourself that allow you to care for your mental, emotional, spiritual, and physical health. Contributing to these things in positive ways can increase the well being of your life. Healthy habits dont only have to do with the things you do, its action you take everyday to better yourself, so some examples of this can be setting boundaries in your life, journaling daily, or even taking steps to eating better.

Impacts
